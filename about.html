Who We Are ?

Craffi, the Karigar is a concept where you can explore and learn about the dynamics of Indian handicrafts. The mission of Craffi, the Karigar is to celebrate and preserve the tales of craft whilst educating the reader on its procedure and export quotient. We value the dignity of handmade crafts, the long hours spent by artisans and the traditional that makes culture an integral part of India. For centuries, Indian handicrafts have been made with love employing locally produced materials and skills mastered by generations. Craffi presents the stories from the by lanes of artist community. Browse through the pages of Indian history and you will be greeted by the rich culture of crafts. Sometimes introduced by kings and sometimes by God, crafts have been instrumental in penning the history of India. They are done both for pleasure and religious purposes and yet their story remains clandestine. At Craffi, we brush off such layers of dust and present tales that strike a conversation with the artist in you!!

Today, handicrafts have become one of the fastest emerging sector of India and play a major role in Indian exports and foreign income. Indian handicrafts include a wide variety of work such as art-metalware, woodware, marble artefacts, sculptures, jewellery, antiques, paintings etc.

Craffi focuses on bringing out the hidden stories from each state of India and showcase it to the world. Over centuries, the craft tradition of India has evolved and has put sparkles of beauty, dignity, religion, mystic form and style around it. At Craffi, we offer plethora of information about the art and crafts of India. A wonderful world of crafts is awaiting you! Come, be charmed by the true beauty and character of Indian handicraft.
